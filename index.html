Welcome to Picstome ​

Picstome is a free, open-source photography service designed to make your photographer's life easier. Self-hosted and packed with features like client galleries, proofing, contract signing, and more!

Features of Picstome ​

Picstome offers a comprehensive set of tools for photographers to manage their galleries, clients, and contracts, all in one place.

Features: ​

  • Free Open Source: Completely free and open-source software.
  • Self-hosted: You control your data.
  • Multilingual: Translate your texts to any language.
  • Low requirements: Runs smoothly on a VPS with low resources.
  • Flexible Storage: Choose between local or S3 storage.
